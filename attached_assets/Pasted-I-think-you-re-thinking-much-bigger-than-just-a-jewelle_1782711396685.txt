# Vision

Sriswa Commerce OS is a self-hosted, AI-ready Commerce Operating System that enables anyone to build, manage, and grow an e-commerce business without writing code or relying on third-party SaaS platforms.

It combines the best ideas from Shopify, WooCommerce, Elementor, Webflow, Framer, and modern analytics dashboards into one unified platform that runs on your own VPS.

## One final recommendation

If you're investing time into this, **don't name everything "Sriswa."**

Instead, separate the platform from the store:

* **Platform:** **Commerce OS** (or another independent name)
* **Store:** **Sriswa Studio**

That way:

* **Sriswa Studio** becomes your jewellery brand.
* **Commerce OS** becomes a reusable e-commerce platform that you can later use for clothing, cosmetics, electronics, or even license to other businesses.

This separation gives you much more flexibility if you ever decide to turn the platform itself into a product.
